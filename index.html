About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio page!

I was born and raised on the southwest coast of Norway, where i lived for the vast majority of my life before i started univeristy to pursue my interest in IT in 2017. During my time at univeristy i had the chance to work with several differnet coding languages, along with some of the different tools and softwares that one might use working with them.

After i finished university in June 2020 i relocated to Northern Ireland, and have been living here ever since. I am currently located in Co.Antrim and living here with my new family of 4 (soon 5).

Until now i have been working as a technical support specialist, while working on some projects for my employer on the side, but also some personal ones at home to build upon on my skills & knowledge. I am at a point now where i really want to try to get a job within the development business, be that Software, Web or even Game developement.

Form Building

One of my most recent projects have been creating a multi-page form that sends both an email with the form data to a set address, and also fills this same data into a google sheet so it can easily be maintained.

I have utilized a couple of tools for this, one being "Parserr" to get the data automatically filled into the google sheet. The form is not yet live but will be hosted on a HTML page on shopify once it goes up.

Basic Android APP

The SAPP app was a test i made using AndroidStudio. The idea was just take an idea and try to bring it to life in the simplest way possible.

The app requires you to create an account that is stored locally on the device, after which you can enter items you would like to purchase and they cost. You also have to option to put in money which then will update a progress bar for each product to showcase how close you are to being able to purchase each item. You will get a notification when you have enough for an item and any items marked as "purchased" are saved in an archive. All the data is stored locally, and comes with different language options programmed in.
